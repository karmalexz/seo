Best Glasses for Round Face (Male and Female): Frames, Shapes and Types

The best glasses for a round face are rectangle, square, geometric, cat-eye, browline and full rimmed glasses, as they provide structure, enhance the features and elongate the face.

A round shaped face is identified by its circular, softer, characteristics, including smooth lines, full cheeks and wide cheekbones. With a round shaped face, the length of the face is approximately equal to the width of the face. The forehead and jawline are likely to measure the same and the hairline is wider than most other face shapes. The frame shapes that are the best fit for a round face are the ones that provide angles and strong lines, bringing more definition and structure to the face. Round glasses for round faces are not recommended as they do not create angles or give sharper structure to the face.
