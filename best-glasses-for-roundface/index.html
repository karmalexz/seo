How to Understand whether you have a Round face or not?

To find out whether or not a person has a round face or not, the face needs to be measured and the shape of the facial features considered. To find out the shape of a face when measuring it, there are four main areas to estimate; the forehead, the cheekbones, the jaw line and the length of the face from chin to hairline. If the measurements across the face are somewhat equal to the measurements from chin to hairline, it is likely that the face is round. It is a good idea to consider the other defining characteristics of a round face when making this assessment. A round face may have softer, less defined features such as a soft jaw and nose. A round face may have full cheeks and wider cheekbones.

To determine if you have a round face you should do the following.

  1. Measure the length of your face: Take a measuring tool like a tape measure and estimate the length of your face from the tip of the chin to the centre of the hairline.
  2. Measure the width of your face: Take the same tool and measure across the forehead. Do the same for the jawline and the cheekbones.If all of these measurements are around the same, it may be an indication that you have a round face.
  3. You should next consider the defining characteristics of a round face and if you may have them.
  4. Consider: Is your face circular, are the features soft, is your jawline rounded and less defined, do you have a fullness in the cheeks. If the answer to these questions are yes, it is likely that you have a round face.

What are the Other Face Shapes for Glasses besides round?
